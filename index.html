How we work

A process with no mystery in it.

Fixed scope, visible progress, working software every two weeks.

  1. Week 1

    Audit

    A fixed-fee teardown of your storefront, ads, and email. We find the money leaks and show you exactly where they are.

  2. Week 2

    Roadmap

    A prioritized plan with effort and payback estimates for every line item. You approve the order before we build anything.

  3. 2-week sprints

    Build

    Working software demoed every other week. No status theater — you see the thing running, or you hear why it isn't.

  4. Ongoing

    Scale

    Monthly reporting against margin, LTV, and repeat rate. We double down on what's working and kill what isn't.

Trust & security

FAQ

Fair questions.

How do engagements start?

Every engagement starts with a fixed-fee audit in week one. You get a written teardown of your storefront, ads, and email — and you keep it whether or not we work together afterward.

What does it cost?

The audit is a flat fee quoted up front. Build work is scoped per sprint, and ongoing management is a monthly retainer tied to defined deliverables. No percentage-of-spend pricing, no surprise line items.

Do you work with brands outside the US?

Yes. We're based in Ohio and work remotely with e-commerce brands across North America and Europe. All meetings are scheduled in your timezone.

Who owns the work?

You do. Code, ad accounts, email lists, dashboards — everything lives in accounts you own from day one, and offboarding is documented when an engagement ends.

Do you lock clients into long contracts?

No. Ongoing work runs month to month after the initial roadmap. We'd rather keep you with results than with paperwork.
