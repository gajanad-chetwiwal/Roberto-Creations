Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026

ROBERTO CREATIONS LLC provides professional services to business clients. This policy explains how cancellations and refunds are handled. It applies alongside the Terms of Service and any signed statement of work.

What we sell

We sell professional services only — website development, digital advertising management, marketing consulting, and related work. We do not sell physical goods, and we do not ship products.

Fixed-fee audits

Audit engagements are billed as a one-time fixed fee agreed in writing before work begins. Because the deliverable is a written analysis prepared specifically for the client, audit fees are non-refundable once the completed audit has been delivered. If we have not yet begun work, the fee is refunded in full on request.

Project & build work

Project work is scoped and invoiced per a written statement of work, typically in stages. Clients may cancel a project in writing at any time. On cancellation:

  • Work completed and delivered up to the cancellation date is billable and non-refundable.
  • Any prepaid amounts for work not yet started are refunded in full.
  • Deliverables completed and paid for are handed over to the client.

Monthly retainers

Ongoing management is billed monthly in advance and runs month to month with no long-term lock-in. Either party may cancel with written notice before the next billing date. The current month is not pro-rated, as the work is performed across the full month; no further invoices are issued after cancellation takes effect.

Third-party costs

Advertising spend, software subscriptions, and platform fees paid to third parties (for example Meta, Google, Shopify, or Klaviyo) are billed directly to the client by those providers or passed through at cost. These amounts are not refundable by us, as they are paid to and controlled by the third party.

Requesting a refund

Refund requests should be sent in writing to support@robertocreationsllc.com. We acknowledge every request within one business day and aim to resolve it within five business days. Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method.

Disputes

If you are unhappy with the work, contact us before raising a dispute with your bank or card issuer. Most issues are resolved directly and quickly. We keep written records of scope, approvals, and deliverables for every engagement.
